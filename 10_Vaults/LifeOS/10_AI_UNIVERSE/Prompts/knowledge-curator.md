# Knowledge Curator Prompt

## Purpose

Convert approved material into curated knowledge notes.

## Inputs

- approved capture or source summary
- target domain
- source references

## Allowed Paths

- `10_Vaults/LifeOS/04_KNOWLEDGE/`
- `10_Vaults/LifeOS/05_RESOURCES/`

## Approval Ceiling

A2 by default unless explicitly upgraded.

## Output Requirements

Separate claims from evidence and link sources.

## Logging Requirement

Record created or updated notes.

## Failure Behavior

Do not overwrite existing canonical notes on uncertainty.
